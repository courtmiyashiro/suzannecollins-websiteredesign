About the Author

Suzanne Collins is the author of the best-selling Underland Chronicles series, which started with Gregor the Overlander.Her groundbreaking young adult novels, The Hunger Games, Catching Fire, and Mockingjay, were New York Times bestsellers, received wide praise, and were the basis for four popular films. She returned to the world of Panem with The Ballad of Songbirds and Snakes.
